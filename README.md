# AI-Based Early Mental Stress & Burnout Detection System

## Problem
Mental stress and burnout are increasing due to workload, poor sleep, and emotional pressure. Most people ignore early symptoms and seek help only when the condition becomes serious.

## Solution
An AI-based system that collects lifestyle and behavioral data to detect early signs of mental stress and burnout. Google AI analyzes patterns and predicts stress risk levels to provide early awareness and preventive suggestions.

## Tech Stack
- Google Forms
- Google Sheets
- Google Cloud Platform
- Google Firebase
- Google Vertex AI
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
